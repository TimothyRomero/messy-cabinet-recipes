Fish Tacos

Ingredients

Taco

  • fish (Tilapia)
  • shrimp
  • tortillas

Cream

  • 2 teaspoon water
  • 2 teaspoon lemon
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1/3 cup of mayo
  • 2/3 cup of Mexican cream
  • Pinch of ground pepper
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Hot sauce

Pico de Gallo

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Salt

Batter

  • 1/2 lb of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 eggs (or 3 egg whites)

Directions

  1. Wash and dry fish (or shrimp).
  2. Cover with flour.
  3. Dip in batter.
  4. Deep fry.

Serving Instructions

  1. Place fritters on a tortilla.
  2. Add julienne sliced cabbage on top.
  3. Add pico de gallo salsa.
  4. Pour some cream on the taco.
  5. Enjoy!
