Ricky's Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Eggs
  • Ground black pepper
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Onions, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger root, minced
  • Sesame seeds
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Peanut butter
  • Soy sauce
  • Chicken or shrimp or both

Directions

  1. Start by steaming the desired amount of rice.
  2. On a flat griddle, pour some olive oil.
  3. Pre-scramble the eggs.
  4. Cook the eggs and add black pepper.
  5. Transfer the eggs to a large container.
  6. Fry the chopped green onions.
  7. Transfer the green onions to the large container.
  8. Fry chopped onions.
  9. Transfer the onions to the large container.
  10. Fry finely chopped garlic, finely chopped ginger root, and sesame seeds.
  11. Transfer the veggies to the large container.
  12. Fry frozen peas, carrots, and green beans.
  13. Transfer to container.
  14. Ricky's secret sauceTM: Blend peanut butter and soy sauce in a mini-blender container.
  15. Fry the steam rice with olive oil.
  16. After the rice becomes loose, add Ricky's secret sauceTM:
  17. Add regular soy sauce, the rice should be a nice light brown color.
  18. Fry chicken, shrimp, meat, or a combination of them as the meat in the rice.
  19. Mix all the ingredients in single large container.
  20. Serve warm and enjoy!
